## üèõÔ∏è Zambian Legal System

### Court Hierarchy
1. **Supreme Court** - Highest court
2. **Constitutional Court** - Constitutional matters
3. **Court of Appeal** - Appellate jurisdiction
4. **High Court** - Original and appellate jurisdiction
5. **Subordinate Courts** - Magistrates' courts
6. **Industrial Relations Court** - Employment matters

### Legal Titles
- **SC (Senior Counsel)** - Highest honor for lawyers
- **State Counsel** - Senior government lawyers
- **Advocate** - Qualified practicing lawyer

### Common Practice Areas
- Constitutional & Electoral Law
- Mining & Natural Resources
- Banking & Finance
- Corporate & Commercial Law
- Criminal Law
- Civil Litigation
- Employment Law
- Land & Property Law
